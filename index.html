About Me

Hello, I am Lachlan Watts-Tobin. I am a third-year EECS (Electrical Engineering & Computer Sciences) student at UC Berkeley, pursuing a minor in Mechanical Engineering, who is passionate about digital design and hardware.

I am currently a Hardware Engineering Intern at Pano AI. I am also the Co-Founder and Chief Electrical Engineer of Watts Electronics, where I design analog circuits for guitar effects pedals inspired by vintage hardwired models. On campus, I develop carbon-fiber manufacturing techniques as a CFRP Engineer with Bike Builders of Berkeley, and I design PCB motor controllers and write their firmware as an Electrical Engineer on the Cal Solar Vehicle team. In Summer 2025, I was an Engineering Intern at Vision IV in Yokohama, Japan, where I designed and optimized a high-performance component using experimental materials, and I served as a Mathematics Learning Facilitator in UC Berkeley's PREP program, teaching pre-calculus to roughly 100 incoming engineering students.
